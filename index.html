About ConstructPro

Building excellence since 1998 with passion, expertise, and commitment to quality

Our Story

From humble beginnings to industry leaders

Founded in 1998 by civil engineer Michael Reynolds, ConstructPro began as a small residential construction company in Johannesburg. With a commitment to quality craftsmanship and ethical business practices, we quickly earned a reputation for excellence.

Over the past 25 years, we've expanded our services to include commercial and industrial construction while maintaining our core values. Today, we operate nationwide with a team of 150+ professionals and have completed over 500 projects across all nine provinces.
